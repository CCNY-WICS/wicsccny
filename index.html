Opportunities

WiCS will be having our '19-'20 e-board election during our GBM 2!
We are looking for enthusiastic and driven people to join our e-board team and help WiCS realize its vision! The roles available are:

  • Secretary

    Handle WiCS e-mail correspondence with our partners and our newsletters to members, take meeting notes, organize the e-board communication, and Google Drive.

  • Social Media and Photography Chair

    • Oversee our FB, Instagram, and LinkedIn accounts, post about upcoming events and opportunities, manage members communication on our social media channels.

  • Financial officer

    Manage WiCS finances and expences, secure funding from Student life and other sponsors, plan budget for each semester with Treasure.

  • Freshmen Rep

    Represent underclassmen members of WiCS, market our events to freshmen, propose and execute ideas to engage new generation WiCS members.

We are really excited to have new perspectives and ideas on our e-board and are looking forward to welcoming new team members! If interested, fill out this interest form and we'll get back to you shortly!
